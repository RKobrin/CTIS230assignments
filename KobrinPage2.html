NFL Draft Thoughts

Eagles' Pick (1,21)

The Eagles went against the predictions of many in selecting Jalen Reagor. While his 40 yard dash wasn't impressive at the NFL Combine,
he made splashes at his pro day with a stunning time of 4.28 seconds (hand-timed finish).

I honestly like this pick. While it may have been a bit of a reach, Reagor brings the vertical speed needed to really unlock the 2 TE system that the Eagles love to run.

Eagles' Pick (2,53)

The Eagles again shocked many analysts by selecting Jalen Hurts, a quarterback out of Oklahoma.

I'm going to be honest here. I don't really understand this selection. While I believe Jalen Hurts can eventually be a solid quarterback in
the NFL, the Eagles recently gave Caron Wentz a large extension. This pick could have been better used on a corner, linebacker, or wide reciever.
